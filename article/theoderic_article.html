Theoderic II Brought Before Pope Ivan

King Theoderic II was brought to Pope Ivan for his laste ceremony.

The King is brought unto his final resting place.

by Matias Kopecky

Updated 1 day ago

After three days of mourning, King Theoderic II of Wescoln at last brought to be set into the earthe by the authoritee of Pope Ivan. He dyed two nyghtes ago of olde age, and ‘tis said to have been peacefully in his slumber. He wast fulle of years, the age of 71, and outlived his Queen Bernhilda I and his sone, Prince Gerland III, who both dyed during the Great War.

He ascended to the throne in the year of 1381 when King Rhesus III passed from pestilence. That marketh 43 years as King of Wescoln. The funerall ceremony wast private, and only those neere to King Theoderic were allowed be therpresent.

– We offer condoleances to the Wescolnian people in this season of passage, says Chancellor Urahe of Teraga, who hath assumed governance after their late Chancellor Zephi, passed a year ago.

This marketh a new era for Wescoln and Teraga, as Chancellor Zephi and King Theoderic II led their nations against one another in the Great War. The Kalderpeople of the North hath not bestowed their condolences to dearest liege lord, the King.

– King Theoderic was most worthy King, his faythfullness to our nation’s greatness shall never be forgotten, says Helga of Dotheim, a wydow since the war.

He will be buried alongside Bernhilda, as Prince Gerland’s corpse was never retrievable.
